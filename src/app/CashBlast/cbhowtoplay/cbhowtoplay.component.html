Tips & Tricks

  1. This is a very entertaining and exciting multiplayer Quiz game where you can complete for Cash Prizes while playing with other Players in real-time. There is no limit to how many times you can play or how much one can win*.
  2. The objective of this game is to answer Qui/Trivia questions correctly. Each player has 3 lives and each wrong answer or not answering in the time given means a loss of all 3 lives the player loses the game and is removed from the table he is playing the game with other players.
  3. The last player left with lives intact is the winner and will win a Cash Amount in the table which is the amount if entry free x number of players on that table and deductions are made for Bandwidth and Platform fee by Mindhunter games.
  4. You can play the free version available for a limited time to get an idea of how the game works or see our Video here [Video](hyperlink)
  5. To play the Cash Prize version. You will have to choose which ‘table’ You want to play on.
  6. The tables are basis the ‘entry fee’.
  7. Eg. In a Rs. 10 table every player (either 2 or max 5 per table) each player would have paid Rs.10 to play and roughly around the same time chosen to play and are randomly placed on a table. In this case the total winning amount for 5 players is Rs.50 and the winner (the last player left in the game) will get Rs.45 which is Rs.50 less the Platform and Bandwidth fee charged by Mindhunter games.
